Welcome to Viljoenskroon Truck Stop

Viljoenskroon Truck Stop is a new truck stop situated in Viljoenskroon, Free State in the industrial area with easy accessibility. The truck stop is security fenced, with security lights at night and 24/7 on-site security. Clean restrooms is on a high standard, and a braai area is also available for relaxation. Trucks can check their loads on a weighbridge which is situated on the premises.
